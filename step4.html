Step 4 - Using/Maintaining the Device

This humidifier requires daily and weekly maintenance to operate appropriately:

  • This is an electrical appliance that requires attention when in use.
  • DO NOT attempt to move, tilt, empty, or refill the device while it is operating, plugged in, or before it has fully cooled.
  • DO NOT block air inlet or outlet while the device is operating.
  • Humidifier should be unplugged when not in use.
  • DO NOT operate if the humidity in the room is 50% or greater.
  • DO NOT operate the device without water in the tank.
  • DO NOT empty or fill the water tank while the devie is plugged in.
  • When cleaning the device, only use cleaners and additives recommended by the manufacturer.
  • DO NOT use detergents, gasoline, glass cleaner, furniture polish, paint thinner, or other household solvents to clean any part of the humidifier.
