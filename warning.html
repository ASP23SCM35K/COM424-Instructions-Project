! IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION !

This product has been safety tested, however, it is still important to keep the following in mind:

To avoid personal injury and/or property damage:

  1. NOTE: DO NOT use the device for anything other than what is decribed in this guide.
  2. NOTE: DO NOT use any attachments not recommended by the manufacturer.
  3. NOTE: DO NOT operate the device if it has been dropped or damaged in any manner.
  4. NOTE: DO NOT attmept to adjust or repair any electrical or mechanical functions on this unit. Doing so will void your warranty. The inside of the unit contains no user serviceable parts.
  5. NOTE: Keep the device dry, indoors, and away from heat sources.
  6. NOTE: DO NOT use in a closed room or face the moisture outlet towards walls, furniture, or anything that can be damaged by moisture.
  7. NOTE: DO NOT operate if the humidity in the room is 50% or greater.
  8. NOTE: DO NOT put anything over the electrical cord and keep it out of high-traffic areas.
  9. NOTE: This is an electrical appliance that requires attention when in use. Keep out of reach of children.
  10. NOTE: DO NOT block air inlet or outlet while the device is operating.
  11. NOTE: DO NOT attempt to move, tilt, empty, or refill the device while it is operating, plugged in, or before it has fully cooled.
