Step 1 - Place the device

To prevent property damage and/or personal injury, the device should NOT be placed:

  • outdoors, under water flow, or immersed in liquids.
  • near heat sources such as stoves, radiators, and heaters.
  • under overhanging surfaces.
  • on a rug or carpet.
  • on a finished floor that may be damaged by exposure to water or moisture.
  • such that the moisture outlet directly faces a wall or anything susceptible to water damage.
  • within reach of children.

For best results, the device should be placed:

  • in an open room
  • on a surface that is:
    • dry
    • firm
    • flat
    • level
  • on a waterproof mat or pad.
  • in an area with low foot traffic
  • at least 4 inches (10cm) away from the wall.
  • within reach of an electical outlet
