Step 3 - Plug in the Device

To prevent personal injury:

  • Do not plug in the cord with wet hands: electric shock could result.
  • Keep the cord out of heavy traffic areas: tripping could result.
  • To avoid fire hazard, plug the appliance directly into a 120V AC electrical outlet or an extension cord with a rating higher than that of the device.
  • DO NOT put anything, including rugs, over the cord.
  • DO NOT place the cord near heat sources.
  • DO NOT attempt to modify the plug or defeat the polarized feature of the plug in any way.
